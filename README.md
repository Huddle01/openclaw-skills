## 🛠 What are OpenClaw Skills?

The Claw community is built different. We're hackers, tinkerers, and builders. OpenClaw Skills are the specialized modules you plug into your OpenClaw agents to give them new superpowers—whether that's fetching data, orchestrating tasks, or taking over the world (ethically, of course).
